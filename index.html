How It Works

1) Submit

Creators submit their web series via FilmFreeway using the official festival page.

2) Review

Entries are reviewed for eligibility and assessed by the festival jury.

3) Recognize

Selected projects receive awards, recognition, and visibility through QuickFlick TV.

Awards & Recognition

Best Web Series

Honors the strongest overall series. Winner receives a featured spotlight on QuickFlick TV and festival social channels.

Best Pilot / Episode

Recognizes an exceptional single episode. Selected for a festival showcase slot and promoted as a standout entry.

Audience / Special Recognition

Celebrates a fan favorite or a unique creative achievement. Includes a special mention and additional visibility during festival week.

About the Festival

Mission

QuickFlick Web Series Festival is a global, digital celebration of web-first storytelling. We spotlight bold episodic voices—from first-time creators to established teams—across every genre and format. Our goal is simple: make discovery easier, honor standout craft, and connect audiences with the next wave of series worth watching.
